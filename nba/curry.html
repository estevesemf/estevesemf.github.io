About Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry, whose full name is Wardell Stephen Curry II, is a professional basketball player in the National Basketball Association (NBA). He is widely regarded as one of the greatest basketball players of his generation and is known for his incredible shooting ability, particularly from long-range three-point shots.
