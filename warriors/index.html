Story

Why Curry changed the geometry of the game

Range as pressure

Curry forced defenses to stretch well beyond the arc. That single change created more space, more motion, and a new rhythm for modern offenses.

Movement without the ball

His impact never depended only on isolation. Relocation, timing, and constant movement made every possession feel active and dangerous.

Identity and atmosphere

Beyond stats, Curry carries a recognizable visual language: confidence, speed, shot creation, and a style that feels cinematic in highlight form.
